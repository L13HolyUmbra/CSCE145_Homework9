The Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe.
Put 3 quarters of a cup of butter in a mixing bowl.
Add 1 quarter cup of lard.
Cream the butter and lard.
Add 1 cup of white sugar.
Add 1 cup of brown sugar.
Mix thoroughly.
Add 2 eggs (without the shells, Ha, Ha, Ha).
Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla.
Mix the batter thoroughly.
Put 2 cups of unbleached flour in another bowl.
Add 1 teaspoon of baking powder to the flour.
Add 1 teaspoon of salt to the flour.
Add 1 teaspoon of baking soda to the flour.
Add 2 and one half cups of thoroughly blended oatmeal to the flour.
Mix the flour ingredients well.
Add the flour mixture to the batter and mix well.
Add 6 ounces of a finely grated chocolate bar to the batter.
Add 12 ounces of chocolate chips.
Mix well.
As an option mix in 1 and one half cups of chopped nuts.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.
Eat the cookies.
